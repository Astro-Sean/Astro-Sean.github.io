Home · Publications (first author)

Peer-reviewed works where I was first author (I led analysis, writing, and coordination unless noted). Each page links to the journal or arXiv version and summarizes the science. Where arXiv HTML figures are stable, there are links to view them (inline /xN.png hotlinks were dropped because they often break).

  • Spectroscopic observations of progenitor activity 100 days before a Type Ibn supernova (SN 2023fyq)

    A&A 684, L18 (2024)

    ZTF light curves plus NOT/Keck/Gemini spectra of a Type Ibn SN with long-lived precursor emission and complex He I evolution before core collapse, with evidence for asymmetric CSM and a genuine explosion.

  • Precursor Activity Preceding Interacting Supernovae I: Bridging the Gap with SN 2022mop

    Submitted to A&A · arXiv:2503.08768

    Multi-epoch follow-up of a precursor in IC 1496 and the later interacting SN at the same site, connecting pre-explosion variability to the eventual Type IIn eruption.

  • SN 2021adxl: A luminous nearby interacting supernova in an extremely low-metallicity environment

    A&A 690, A259 (2024)

    A luminous nearby SN IIn in a very metal-poor host: photometry, spectra, and environment to constrain interaction and progenitor context at low Z.

  • The Automated Photometry of Transients pipeline (AUTOPHOT)

    A&A 667, A62 (2022)

    Methods paper: Python pipeline for forced photometry, uncertainty handling, and survey-style transient follow-up (used across several of my observational projects).

  • The impostor revealed: SN 2016jbu was a terminal explosion

    A&A 664, L18 (2022)

    Letter arguing that SN/AT 2016jbu was a core-collapse SN rather than a non-terminal impostor, using spectral evolution and late-time confirmation.

  • Photometric and spectroscopic evolution of the interacting transient AT 2016jbu (Gaia16cfr)

    MNRAS 513, 5642 (2022)

    First of two AT 2016jbu papers: multi-colour light curves and spectral sequence for this long-lived interacting transient (with a companion paper on progenitor and modelling).

  • Progenitor, environment, and modelling of the interacting transient AT 2016jbu (Gaia16cfr)

    MNRAS 513, 5666 (2022)

    Companion study: constraints on progenitor and CSM from modelling and host environment for AT 2016jbu.
